Fettuccine

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: hz
Ingredients Quantity
8 ounces of fettuccine pasta about 225g
1/2 cup unsalted butter 1 stick
Heavy cream 1 cup
grated Parmesan cheese 1 cup

Step 1: Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.

Step 3: Pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly to combine with the melted butter.

Step 4: Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously to create a smooth and creamy sauce.

Step 5: Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the thickness of the sauce by adding more cream or cheese as desired.

Step 6: Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade Fettuccine Alfredo!

Sushi

  • Time: 2 hour
  • Serves: hz
Ingredients Quantity
Sushi rice 2 sups
Water 1 cup
Rice vinegar 1/3 cup
tablespoons sugar 3

Step 1: Place a bamboo sushi rolling mat on a flat surface and put a sheet of nori on top, shiny side down.

Step 2: Wet your hands and take a handful of sushi rice, spreading it evenly over the nori, leaving a small border at the top.

Step 3: Arrange thin slices of fish, avocado, and cucumber along the bottom edge of the rice.

Step 4: Using the bamboo mat, roll the nori tightly over the filling, sealing the edge with a little water.

Step 5: Use a sharp knife to slice the roll into bite-sized pieces.

Step 6: Serve the sushi rolls with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.
